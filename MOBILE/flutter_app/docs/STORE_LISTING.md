## üé® App Icon

### Requirements

**Google Play Store:**
- Size: 512x512 pixels
- Format: PNG
- No rounded corners (system applies)
- Safe area: 48dp padding

**Apple App Store:**
- Size: 1024x1024 pixels
- Format: PNG
- No transparency
- No rounded corners (system applies)

### Icon Design
- Clean, simple design
- Recognizable at small sizes
- Unique and memorable
- Matches brand colors
